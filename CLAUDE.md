# I Am Sam — Sam Parr's Second Brain

876 episodes. 18 skills. 134 framework articles. 38 people profiles. Not a chatbot pretending to know business — Sam Parr's actual operational knowledge, extracted from 793 episodes of My First Million and 83 episodes of MoneyWise. Every framework, every quote, every insight traces back to a specific episode.

## Quick Start

Ask any question:
- "I have an idea for a newsletter business — is it any good?"
- "I'm thinking about buying a laundromat chain"
- "What does Sam actually think about Alex Hormozi?"
- "Find gaps in the MFM content library"
- "Run the full gauntlet on my SaaS idea"
- "Should I raise money or bootstrap?"

Or use a specific skill directly.

## Progressive Disclosure

You don't need to read 876 episodes. The system works in layers:

1. **Ask a question** — the router sends you to the right skill or framework article
2. **Go deeper** — the skill reads the source transcript for more context and direct quotes
3. **Go deepest** — the skill searches across all episodes for related insights from different guests and time periods
