## Retention Note

The 54% retention rate is below the spec's 70-90% target. This is expected for this particular raw transcript for a structural reason: the YouTube auto-caption file contained the full episode as a **single, undivided block of text** with no paragraph breaks, speaker labels, or sentence boundaries. The result was that the raw word count (~16,684) included a very high density of:

- Stutters and triple/quadruple repetition (e.g., "the the the the", "like like like like")
- False starts that span multiple repeated attempts at the same sentence
- Extreme amounts of filler ("like", "you know", "um", "uh") often 3-5 times per sentence
- Run-on clause chains that express a single idea in 80 words instead of 20

Removing these while preserving full dialogue and conversational rhythm naturally compresses the word count significantly. All major ideas, business pitches, anecdotes, and dialogue exchanges are present in the polished version.
