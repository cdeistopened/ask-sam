**Doug:** Yeah, that feels right. I should always know the numbers, but yeah.

**Doug:** Yeah, that's right. The first car was my car. I've since sold four or five — although I haven't bought yet, I gotta do that.

## The Creator Business Model: Can PE Own a YouTuber? [00:40:00]
