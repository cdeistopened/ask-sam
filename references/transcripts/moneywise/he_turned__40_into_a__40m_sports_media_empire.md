This episode introduces a new format for the MoneyWise podcast, shifting focus from personal finance transparency to company building. Sam Parr interviews Adam White, founder of Front Office Sports, who shares his strategies for building a $40 million sports media empire. **Topics:** Podcast format change, company building, brand strategy, cohesive branding, premium branding, Forbes 30 Under 30, Rising 25 award, brand presence, executive retreats, blimp advertising, growth frameworks, weekly performance meetings, Jeff Zucker, confidence levels, revenue goals, C90, C60, C10, tracking bookings, hiring strategy, Culture Index, C-Job, emotionally healthy kids, entrepreneurship, Hampton community.
