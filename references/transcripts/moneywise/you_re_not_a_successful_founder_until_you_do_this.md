There is such a thing as financial success or business success, or even I read the four books or I didn't read the four books. Uh, you can say I hit a million dollars, I am successful now, or maybe you just stay true to those metrics and it feels good and you don't feel like an imposter or whatnot. That's all good. Those things exist and they are versions of success, for sure. But the recurring problem I've noticed, and the thing that a lot of the founders that we've spoken to have had to overcome, is when you do confuse those two things and those more materialistic, for lack of better words, milestones become your version of success and what you're looking for fulfillment in.
