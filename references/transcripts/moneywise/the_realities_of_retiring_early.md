## Summary
In this episode of the MoneyWise podcast, hosts Sam Parr and Harry Morton interview two anonymous guests, "Ryan" and "Jeff," who both achieved significant financial success at a young age and chose to retire early. The discussion dives into the psychological challenges of early retirement, the loss of identity and purpose, and how these individuals navigate life after a major exit.

**Topics:** Early retirement, financial independence, identity, purpose, mental health, wealth management, lifestyle design, entrepreneurship, post-exit life.

**Speakers:** Sam Parr, Harry Morton, Ryan (Guest), Jeff (Guest)
