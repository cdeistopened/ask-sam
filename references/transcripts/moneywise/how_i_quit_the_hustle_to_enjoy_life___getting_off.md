## Episode Summary
In this episode, entrepreneur Laura Roeder discusses her journey from building and selling a successful SaaS business to achieving financial independence and shifting her focus toward lifestyle design. She shares insights on the importance of defining "enough," the role of a supportive partner, and the mindset shift required to move from constant growth to intentional living.

**Topics:** Financial Independence, Entrepreneurship, Lifestyle Design, Wealth Management, SaaS, Exit Strategy, Work-Life Balance
**Speakers:** Sam Parr, Harry Morton, Laura Roeder
