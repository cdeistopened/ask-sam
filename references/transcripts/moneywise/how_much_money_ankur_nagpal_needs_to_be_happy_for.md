## Defining the Threshold [03:45]

**Ankur Nagpal:** Now, to be fair, I think there is a range for our threshold number. There was this 2010 study that people were citing for years saying that money stops impacting your happiness around $75,000 a year, which is around 106 grand in 2023 dollars. And guess what? I think it's nonsense. $100,000 is not the threshold number for most everyone in America. Since then, numerous studies have contradicted this and concluded that there was no plateau in happiness when it comes to income. But my point is that money's impact on happiness depends on each individual's general outlook and feelings on life. But if you have your act together, more money will equal more happiness, which means there is a threshold number where one begins feeling like they have enough. Ankur's answer to our question of how much is enough needs to be understood through the perspective of his upbringing.
